    Usage: *

            The last major projection proposed by Johann Heinrich Lambert was the Lambert Azimuthal Equal-Area projection. Also known as the Zenithal or simply Azimuthal Equal-Area projection, the Lambert Azimuthal Equal-Area projection has equal area and the azimuthal property of showing true direction from the center of this projection. Scale varies by less at any given distance from the center of the projection than the scale of any other azmuthals.

            A common variation of the Lambert Azmuthal is called Hammer or Hammer-Aitoff.

     * Usage information source: Map Projections A Working Manual, USGS Proffesional Paper 1395, John P. Snyder
