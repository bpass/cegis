Decision Support System for Map Projections of Small Scale Data

  • Usage

    • Lambert Conformal Conic

    Usage: *

            Officially adopted projection for the State Plane Coordinate Systems (SPCS) of states of predominantly east-west expanse, the Lambert Conformal Conic preserves scale along one or two chosen parallels. Additionally, the Lambert Conformal Conic is used for the 1:1,000,000-scale regional world aeronautical charts, the 1:500,000-scale sectional aeronautical charts, and the 1:500,000-scale State base maps(all 48 mainland States have the same standard parallels of lat. 33 ° and 44 ° N., and thus match).

     * Usage information source: Map Projections A Working Manual, USGS Proffesional Paper 1395, John P. Snyder
